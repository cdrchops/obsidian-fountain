Index Cards — Section Editing Prototype

Static prototype — no Fountain parser, mock JS data.
  • Pencil on section header → rename. The input shows ## Title; edit either the #s or the title. Depth changes do not cascade — children keep their depth. Clearing the input deletes the section header and promotes its children to the parent.
  • Horizontal + section bar appears in two places only: above the first section (when there are no preceding root scenes) and at the bottom of the doc. Sections between adjacent scene cards are reachable via the vertical gutter's # button instead.
  • Hover left of any scene card → vertical bar with + (scene) and # (section). The # splits the parent section: everything from the click point onwards becomes the content of a new sibling section. Also a scene-drag drop target.
  • Hover right of the last scene in a section → same vertical bar (its own hover, separate from the card's body). Inserts at the boundary between this section's direct scenes and any nested subsection.
  • Empty section (Act Two) shows a dashed + card — visible at all times so you have something to aim at.
  • Hover the bottom of the doc → same + section bar for tail-of-doc inserts. Empty doc shows just the dashed + card.
  • Section drag-reorder is intentionally not implemented in this iteration — pending more thought.
